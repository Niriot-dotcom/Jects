You should to add the library "Sound" in your Processing.

How?
   1. Go to "Sketch" in the tool bar.
   2. Click in "Import library..." and next in "Add library..."
   3. Search "Sound" and select the library with autor "The 
      Processing Foundation".
   4. Click in "Install" button and wait.
   5. Ready, you can play now!
